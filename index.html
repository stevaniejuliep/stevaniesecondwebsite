INI ADALAH HALAMAN KARAKTER

  • Snoopy was created by Charles M. Schulz in 1950 for the Peanuts comic strip.
  • Charlie Brown’s pet beagle
  • Snoopy is loyal, clever, and a bit cheeky
  • He often mocks Charlie Brown but clearly loves him
  • He’s obsessed with cookies, especially chocolate chip (which, unlike real dogs, doesn’t harm him in the comic universe)
